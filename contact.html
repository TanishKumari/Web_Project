FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Medixo.

How do I book my first appointment?
Simply fill out the contact form above or click "Book Appointment" in the navigation. You can also call us directly at +1 (800) 624-9460. Our care coordinator will help you find the right specialist.
Is Medixo available in my country?
We serve patients in 40+ countries. Online consultations are available globally. Lab tests, medicine delivery, and home care services are available in select regions. Contact us to check availability in your area.
Do you accept insurance?
Yes! We work with most major insurance providers. Upload your insurance card during registration and we'll verify your coverage. We also offer direct-pay options at transparent rates for uninsured patients.
How secure is my health data?
Your health data is protected with AES-256 encryption, HIPAA compliance, and strict access controls. We never sell your data or share it without your explicit consent. Your privacy is our absolute priority.
What happens in a medical emergency?
For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 first. For urgent (non-emergency) medical concerns, call our 24/7 emergency line at +1 (800) 911-2468 and we'll connect you with an on-call physician immediately.
Can I get prescriptions through Medixo?
Yes. Our licensed physicians can issue electronic prescriptions during your consultation. Prescriptions are sent directly to your preferred pharmacy or fulfilled through our medicine delivery service for doorstep delivery.
