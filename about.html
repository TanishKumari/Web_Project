Our Story

Healthcare That Puts
Patients First

Medixo was born from a simple but powerful belief: that every person deserves access to quality healthcare — regardless of where they live or their financial situation. Founded by Dr. James Mitchell and technologist Priya Sharma in 2009, we started as a small telehealth clinic in New York City.

Today, we're a full-scale digital health platform serving patients across 40+ countries, with a network of over 200 verified specialists spanning 25 medical disciplines. Our technology and compassionate care combine to deliver health outcomes that truly matter.

Purpose & Direction

Guided by Mission,

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality healthcare by leveraging technology to connect every patient with the right care — at the right time, at the right cost. We break down barriers of geography, cost, and complexity to make health accessible to all.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where preventive care is the norm, chronic disease is the exception, and every person on earth has a trusted digital health partner in their pocket. We envision a future where health outcomes are driven by data, empathy, and innovation.

💎

Our Promise

We promise unwavering dedication to patient safety, privacy, and dignity. Every decision we make is guided by clinical excellence, ethical standards, and a deep respect for the trust our patients place in us every single day.

🤝

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, empathy, and excellence. These aren't just words on a wall — they're embedded into every interaction, product decision, and clinical protocol that defines who we are as an organization.

Our Journey

15 Years of Innovation

From a small NYC clinic to a global healthcare platform.

2009

Founded in New York City

Dr. James Mitchell and Priya Sharma launch Medixo as a telehealth pilot program serving 500 patients in NYC.

2012

Series A Funding — $8M Raised

Secured $8M in Series A funding, expanded to 5 states and launched the mobile application for iOS and Android.

2015

100 Specialists Onboarded

Reached a major milestone — 100 verified specialists across 20 disciplines and 10,000+ registered patients.

2018

International Expansion

Expanded into Canada, UK, and Australia. Launched the Family Health Plan and partnered with 3 major insurance providers.

2021

AI Health Assistant Launched

Introduced Medixo AI — a symptom checker and health navigator powered by clinically validated machine learning models.

2024

50,000+ Patients & 40+ Countries

Reached global scale. Named Best Digital Health Platform 2024. Serving patients across 40+ countries with 200+ specialists.
